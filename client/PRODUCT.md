# PRODUCT.md

## Product Purpose
Real-time multiplayer companion app for running Dungeon Crawler Carl tabletop RPG sessions. Replaces paper character sheets with a shared, synced HUD. GM controls HP, Room Target, loot, and mobs. Players see their character status update in real-time on their own screen.

## Users
- GM (1): Running combat, managing loot, controlling the dungeon. Needs instant access to HP adjustment and Room Target. No fumbling.
- Players (3-4): Checking their own HP, stats, room target, incoming loot boxes. Glance-able at arm's length on a laptop or tablet.

## Register
product

## Tone
Dark, high-energy, slightly sci-fi. Dungeon terminal aesthetic. The UI IS the in-world HUD that crawlers see inside the dungeon. Sardonic AI personality. Everything feels like it's run by a broadcasting corporation that wants you to suffer entertainingly.

## Brand
- Background: near-black #080810
- Panels: #0f0f1a
- Primary accent: electric purple #a855f7 (the System/AI colour)
- Secondary: cyan #22d3ee
- Font: JetBrains Mono throughout
- NO rounded corners, sharp edges only
- Loot tier colours: Bronze #CD7F32, Silver #C0C0C0, Gold #F59E0B, Platinum #60A5FA, Legendary #F97316, Celestial #A855F7

## Anti-references
- Generic dark mode SaaS dashboard
- D&D Beyond (too corporate, too friendly)
- Fantasy map aesthetic (no parchment, no serif fonts)
- Neon cyberpunk cliche

## Strategic principles
- Readability at arm's length on a laptop is non-negotiable
- Every GM action must be reachable in one tap
- The app should feel like it exists inside the DCC universe
- Fun over polish
